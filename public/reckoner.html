Care Services

What care services will you need to pay for?

You may be eligible to have some or all of your care costs paid for you. To find out, we would need to know more about your finances. In which case, we will ask more detailed questions about your savings and income as part of a care assessment.

If you wish to have a care assessment you can either:

  • Click here to access an on line self-assessment form
  • Or click here to speak to someone who will help you with the assessment

Full Costs

It is likely that you will have to pay the full amount of any care services if any of the following are true:

  • You have more than £23,250 in savings.
  • You are part of a couple and you and your partner/spouse have joint saving greater than £46,500.
  • You own a property that you do not live in.
